# Principles

- Placements come before everything else.
- Secure opportunities before optimizing optionality.
- Keep the MS door open without sacrificing placements.
- Health is maintained, not maximized, during placement season.
- Improve existing work before starting something new.
- Consistency beats intensity.
- Build things that are interview-worthy, not just resume-worthy.
- Every major activity should support at least one North Star goal.
- Don't chase achievements just because they're prestigious.
- Make decisions once; review them intentionally instead of repeatedly questioning them.
